# 01 — Design Vision

## Zendo (禅道)

> 禅 (Zen) + 道 (The Way) — The Way of Presence

### Core Philosophy

Zendo is not a productivity tool. It is a **stillness companion**. It borrows from the Japanese concept of **Ma (間)** — the space between things, the pause that gives shape to everything else.

Where other Pomodoro timers shout for attention with gamification, streaks, and urgency, Zendo does the opposite: it is a quiet presence that fades into the periphery until the moment it’s needed. It respects attention, never demands it.

### Three Design Pillars

**1. Seijaku (静寂) — Stillness**
Every visual element exists with intention. Nothing extraneous. The interface has only what's needed in the exact moment, no more. Empty space is treated as a first-class design material.

**2. Shibui (渋い) — Understated Elegance**
No gradients, no shadows, no glass effects, no neon. Beauty comes from proportion, tone, and subtle texture. The design is quiet enough to live permanently on screen without fatigue.

**3. Ima (今) — Right Now**
The timer shows only what matters now. Momentary state. No history, no statistics, no future planning. Just this session, right now.

### Tone & Character

| Dimension | Choice |
|---|---|
| **Aesthetic** | Japanese Zen × Exaggerated Minimalism |
| **Personality** | Calm, centered, unhurried, wise |
| **Contrast level** | High contrast, soft palette |
| **Visual density** | Ultra-sparse — one element per conceptual moment |
| **Emotional response** | "I can breathe." |

### The Unforgettable Detail

The timer ring is not a standard progress circle. It is a **single, hairline ring** drawn with the precision of an enso brush circle — slightly imperfect, organic easing, as if painted in sumi ink. When the session completes, the ring doesn't pop or flash; it **gently dissolves** like ink dispersing in water.

### Anti-Vision (What Zendo Will Never Be)

- No notifications with sound or badges
- No gamification, streaks, or points
- No social sharing
- No statistics dashboard
- No task lists or todo integration
- No motivational messages
- No urgency-inducing countdown alarms
- No emojis, illustrations, or decorative graphics
